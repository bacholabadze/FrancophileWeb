What we offer

Our startup aims to promote and educate the world about Francophone culture through an innovative web and mobile application.

Gamified Learning

With a focus on gamified learning, users can delve into various aspects of culture, history, science, and more.

Integrating E-books

The app caters to students, making the study process flexible by integrating e-books and providing tools to mark upcoming exercises and exams.

Challenges

You can challenge friends and individuals worldwide in different categories, which are generated by AI or contributed by the community.

Our Goal

Our application fosters connections between people, facilitating language learning and providing a platform to explore the rich world of French language, cuisine, music, and culture. Through interactive experiences and engaging challenges, our app becomes an enticing and desirable tool for a wide audience, as our preliminary research has shown.
